Tata Consultancy Services



Tata Consultancy Services, Gandhinagar IT Tower 3 - Our Training Center

I joined TCS on 12th August, 2010 as a Assistant System Engineer - Trainee. It was 3 months training duration and divided into 3 phases. Purpose of phases was to make candidate skilled in

  • Analytical ability
  • Technical knowledge
  • Communication and presentation skills
  • Ability to handle real world problems
  • Ability to give cleaner and optimum solutions
In the first phase I was trained in Core Java. Where I underwent a rigorous training with skilled professors and advanced practical material to strengthen my grip on strong programming practice.

However the purpose of first phase was not only to give candidates technical knowledge but to foster their presentation skills. With the team of best teachers I was also trained on variety of soft skills such as group discussion, debate, stage skills and communication expertise

In the remaining phases of training, I was specifically trained in Mainframe Technology. It gave the insight of various file systems, methodology, protocols and patterns associated with IBM mainframe machines.

After Completion of my training I was transferred to the base branch at Rajashree Business Park situated in Pune on Tadiwala Road.

It was one of the most important period of my life as it gave me first insight in corporate world

In TCS at Rajashree Business Park, I was put in the Banking and Finance Domain where I worked on Debit card transaction processing application. My work areas included Development, Testing and Quality analysis. I also worked on project documentation as well as involved myself in tutorial creation for framework and tools used in Mainframe paradigm
